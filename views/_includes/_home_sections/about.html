About

Over the years I've worked for a range of companies and agencies on everything from small marketing sites to platform rebuilds and responsive email templates.

My love for technology doesn't stop when the clock hits 6pm. Around usual working hours I've made paper circuits things with conductive ink, models for 3D printing, holograms, motion tracking prototypes with Kinect and Leap Motion, and some templates and tools to make my (and maybe some others) development lives a little easier. Anything that works (and sometimes doesn't) will usually appear on my Github account.
