About

I have been working with the web since 2006 after graduating with a bachelors degree in Computer Science from The University of Hertfordshire.

Over the years I've spent time working for myself, for large companies, and various agencies - currently I'm full-time at FullSIX London - which has given me the opportunity to use a wide range of technologies and work on large and small scale builds.

My love for technology doesn't stop when the clock hits 6pm. In and around usual working hours I've also played around with conductive ink, 3D printing, holograms, Kinect development, and have created templates and tools. Anything that works (and sometimes doesn't) will usually appear on Github.
